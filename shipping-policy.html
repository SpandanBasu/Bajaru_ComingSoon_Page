Shipping Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Delivery Method

All orders are delivered by our own delivery partners and riders. We do not use third-party courier services — your order is handled by Bajaru's trusted delivery team from start to finish.

Delivery Timeline

Orders are typically delivered within 2 days from the date of order confirmation, or as per the delivery date agreed at the time of purchase. Actual delivery times may vary based on your location and rider availability.

Delivery Address

Delivery will be made to the address you provide at the time of purchase. Please ensure your address is accurate and complete to avoid delays.

Delivery Costs

Any delivery charges applied at the time of order are non-refundable.

Liability

While we strive for timely delivery, Bajaru is not liable for delays caused by unforeseen circumstances such as weather conditions, traffic, or other factors beyond our control.
