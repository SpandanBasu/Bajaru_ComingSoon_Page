🌿 Our Story

We believe fresh food
is a right, not a luxury

Bajaru is a new initiative focused on connecting local farms directly with customers. Our goal is simple — make fresh, high-quality vegetables accessible while supporting farmers with fair pricing. We are currently preparing for launch and building our farm network to bring truly fresh produce to your doorstep.

Our Mission

Cut the chain.
Keep the freshness.

Many vegetables available in cities travel long distances before reaching customers. Our mission is to shorten this journey by building a direct connection between farms and households.

Bajaru is currently preparing to launch a platform where customers can access fresh vegetables sourced directly from local farms. By reducing the supply chain, we aim to deliver fresher produce while supporting farmers with fair pricing.

Our Values

What we stand for

Every decision at Bajaru comes back to these four principles.

🌍

Sustainability first

From compostable packaging to carbon-neutral deliveries, every choice we make considers the long-term health of our planet.

🤝

Fair for farmers

Farmers earn 40% more per kilo through our platform than through traditional wholesale channels. We believe growers deserve fair pay.

🔍

Radical transparency

Every product listing shows which farm it came from, when it was harvested, and the pesticide test results. No secrets.

❤️

Community driven

We reinvest a portion of every order into local farming education programs and supporting smallholder farmers in adopting organic practices.
