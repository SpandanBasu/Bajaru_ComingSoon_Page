About the AI

Voice-powered.
Everyone can shop.

We've automated the entire app experience so even elderly and non-techy users can order fresh vegetables with ease. Browse, search, add items, check prices, and add your address — all by speaking in your native language.

Just say "Bhaiya 2 kg alu or 1 kg tamatar dedijiye" and boom — your cart is ready. Bajaru works flawlessly in Bengali, Hindi, and English, making fresh food accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

Cut the chain.
Keep the freshness.

Many vegetables available in cities travel long distances before reaching customers. Our mission is to shorten this journey by building a direct connection between farms and households.

Bajaru is currently preparing to launch a platform where customers can access fresh vegetables sourced directly from local farms. By reducing the supply chain, we aim to deliver fresher produce while supporting farmers with fair pricing.

Our Values

What we stand for

Every decision at Bajaru comes back to these four principles.

🌍

Sustainability first

From compostable packaging to carbon-neutral deliveries, every choice we make considers the long-term health of our planet.

🤝

Fair for farmers

Farmers earn 40% more per kilo through our platform than through traditional wholesale channels. We believe growers deserve fair pay.

🤖

AI-powered ordering

Bajaru uses AI to automate the entire ordering process. Just speak your vegetables and the app adds items, checks prices, and prepares your cart instantly.

🥦

Handpicked vegetables

Every vegetable is carefully selected from trusted local farms to ensure freshness, quality, and great taste in every delivery.
